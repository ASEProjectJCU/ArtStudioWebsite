Exhibitions

Lim Cheng Hoe:
Painting Singapore

From 02 Aug 2018 - 09 Jun 2019

This exhibition features over 60 artworks, sketches and archival materials that highlight Lim Cheng Hoe's mastery of outdoor watercolour landscape painting.

FROM CONTEMPORARY SCULPTURE

From 13 Sep 2018 - Jan 3 2019

From Earth and Metal: Contemporary Sculpture is a group sculpture exhibition featuring renowned international artists Bernar Venet, Pablo Reinoso and Jedd Novatt.

ART STAGE 2018:
FABIENNE AND NAN QI

From 03 Oct 2018 - 05 Nov 2018

Exhibitions of French artist Fabienne Verdier and Chinese ink artist Nan Qi. These exhibitions unveiled 5 significant new paintings by Fabienne Verdier.
